# Module 3: Explore

## Insight
Possibility expands when the client feels safe to think freely.

## Powerful Questions
- What are your options?
- What else is possible?
- What would courage look like here?

## Exercise
**Possibility Brainstorm**

1. Think about your current goal or challenge.
2. Set a timer for 5 minutes and write down **at least 10 possible ways** to move forward — include even the ideas that seem far-fetched or unrealistic.
3. Review your list without judgment.
4. Circle the option that excites or energizes you most.
5. Ask yourself: *"What is one small step I can take toward that option this week?"*

> The goal is not to find the perfect answer, but to open the door to new thinking.
