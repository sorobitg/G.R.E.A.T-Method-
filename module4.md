# Module 4: Action

## Insight

Clarity without action creates frustration.
Action creates transformation.

## Coaching Questions

- What will you do next?
- When will you do it?
- What support do you need?

## Exercise

**Your Action Plan**

Use the prompts below to build a concrete, time-bound action plan based on your goal.

1. **One Next Step** – Write down the single most important action you will take toward your goal.

   > _Example: "I will send the proposal email to my top three prospects."_

   My next step: _______________________________________________

2. **Deadline** – Commit to a specific date and time by which you will complete this step.

   My deadline: _______________________________________________

3. **Accountability** – Identify one person who will hold you accountable, and describe how they will check in with you.

   My accountability partner: _______________________________________________

   How they will check in: _______________________________________________

4. **Obstacles & Solutions** – List one potential obstacle and how you plan to overcome it.

   | Obstacle | My Solution |
   |----------|-------------|
   |          |             |

5. **Reflection** – After completing the step, answer these questions:
   - Did I do what I said I would do? (Yes / No / Partially)
   - What did I learn from taking this action?
   - What is my next step now?
