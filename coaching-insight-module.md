# Coaching Insight Module

## Coaching Insight

Clarity is not something you give your client.  
It is something you help them uncover.

---

## Guided Coaching Script

- What do you truly want?
- What is holding you back?
- Where is God leading you right now?

---

## Practice Exercise

**Write your coaching response:**

As your coach, my role is not to hand you answers — it is to walk alongside you as you discover them.

Start by sitting with the first question: *What do you truly want?* Not what others expect of you, not what feels safe or practical — but what stirs something deep inside you. That desire is data. It points toward purpose.

Next, name what is holding you back. Fear, doubt, past failure, the opinions of others — whatever it is, bring it into the light. We cannot move past what we refuse to acknowledge. Naming it gives you power over it.

Finally, pause and ask: *Where is God leading me right now?* Not where you wish He would lead, or where you planned to go — but where He is *actually* moving in your life today. Look for the open doors, the recurring themes, the quiet nudges that keep returning.

When you align your desire with your honest obstacles and your sense of divine direction, clarity begins to emerge — not as something given to you, but as something *you* have uncovered.

---

## Faith Reflection

> "Commit to the Lord whatever you do, and He will establish your plans." – Proverbs 16:3

Commitment precedes clarity. When we surrender our plans to God, He does not simply rubber-stamp them — He *establishes* them. He builds a foundation beneath them. Trust the process. Take the next faithful step.
