# Module 5: Timing

## Insight
Timing turns intention into accountability.

## Questions
- By when will you complete this?
- How will you track progress?
- What will keep you accountable?

## Exercise

Use the table below to create a **Timing Plan** for your goal. For each action step you identified, set a specific deadline, define how you will track it, and name an accountability measure.

**Reflection prompts:**

1. **Commitment date** – Write the single most important milestone and the date you will complete it by:
   > "I will _______________ by _______________."

2. **Progress check-ins** – How often will you review your progress? (daily / weekly / bi-weekly)
   Scheduled check-in day/time: _______________

3. **Accountability partner** – Name one person who will hold you accountable and describe how they will do so:
   > "_______________ will hold me accountable by _______________."

4. **Course-correction** – What will you do if you fall behind schedule?
   > "If I fall behind, I will _______________."
