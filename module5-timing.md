# Module 5: Timing

## Insight

Timing turns intention into accountability.

## Questions

- By when will you complete this?
- How will you track progress?
- What will keep you accountable?

## Exercise

Use the prompts below to create a concrete timeline for your goal.

**1. Set a completion date**
Write down the specific date by which you will achieve your goal.

> My goal is: _______________________________________________
>
> I will complete it by: _______________________________________________

**2. Break it into milestones**
Identify 3–5 key milestones between now and your completion date. Assign a target date to each one.

| Milestone | Target Date |
|-----------|-------------|
| 1.        |             |
| 2.        |             |
| 3.        |             |
| 4.        |             |
| 5.        |             |

**3. Schedule your next action**
What is the very next step you will take, and when exactly will you take it?

> Next action: _______________________________________________
>
> Date & time: _______________________________________________

**4. Choose your accountability method**
Select one or more ways you will track and stay accountable to your timeline:

- [ ] Weekly check-in with a coach or accountability partner
- [ ] Daily journal or progress log
- [ ] Calendar reminders for each milestone
- [ ] Shared goal tracker (app, spreadsheet, or whiteboard)
- [ ] Other: _______________________________________________

**5. Reflect**
Answer the following questions honestly:

- What obstacles might delay your timeline, and how will you handle them?
- What does staying on track look like for you day to day?
- How will you celebrate when you hit each milestone?
